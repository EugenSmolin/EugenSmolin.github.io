In today’s fast-paced global business environment, possessing a comprehensive distribution chain is vital to the survival of a business with an international customer base. Heightened demands associated with shipping, customs, warehousing, inventory control, and distribution can and do take excessive amounts of time, attention, and effort that would otherwise be spent on improving marketing, product quality, and other business aspects of an enterprise. It is our mission to be the vital link in our clients’ distribution chain, allowing them to free up more time to concentrate on their business. We achieve this by using state-of-the-art integrated technology to provide comprehensive logistics solutions for international freight, from shipping and customs clearance of dangerous goods to warehousing, inventory control, and distribution using the latest Internet-based systems.

While we recognize that modern business environment is electronically competitive, we do not forget even for a moment that traditional practices still form the basis for business. Our team of experienced professionals is specifically trained to recognize this synergy and help you take advantage of existing and emerging business opportunities by introducing and developing innovative logistics solutions customized specifically for your business needs. Our commitment to achieve the highest level of customer satisfaction by providing professional and personalized service is at the core of our success model, and we will always strive to meet and exceed your expectations.

The integrity of an import/export industry, especially with finished high-value goods, like automobiles, depends heavily on the timely and responsible handling of their duties by every link in the supply, acquisition, and delivery chain, with the processing facility playing a crucial role.TMM International Company’s operational facility is a 100,000-square-foot site located in Perth Amboy, New Jersey, at a confluence of major shipping routes. This gives us an increased ability to smoothly manage cargo flows from the Americas and Western Europe into and out of the countries of Eastern Europe, the Baltic States, and the Middle East.The location and size of our Terminal contributes a great deal to improving access to major shipping lanes, and the fact that it is surrounded by water adds to security and convenience.

Whether you are shipping domestically or internationally, by ground, water or air, our facility will ensure damage-free transit by providing secure packing and placing your shipment on pallets or in crates – the choice is yours. We transport full truckloads as well as LTL (small shipments). Additional services at our warehousing facility include cross-docking and short-term and long-term storage.

Our business headquarters is located in the heart of the New York Metropolitan area, allowing easy access to dealers located in the major market area of the United States. Moreover, the diverse ethnic background and vast international experience of the management team at TMM International Company gives us a competitive edge by adding to the ability to handle a diverse workforce, as well as better anticipate and fulfill varying requirements put forth by international clients. At every stage of the process, from receipt to on-time delivery, your shipment will be in the care of our well-tuned network.
